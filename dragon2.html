What is Origami Dragon?

In Japan, origami dragons tend to symbolize wisdom and balance rather than raw power.
The dragon has been a prominent symbol in Japanese culture for hundreds of years.
While Chinese dragons tend to symbolize power and strength, Japanese culture values the symbol of the dragon for wisdom and balance.
